About Me

Hi! I’m Ellie Reynolds, a student at Miami University, planning to graduate in 2028, and pursuing a degree in Sport Communication and Media with a focus on sport photography.

Sports have always been at the heart of both my life and my family’s. Growing up in a college town, Miami University, and near Cincinnati, home to our favorite professional teams like the Reds and Bengals, I had countless opportunities to attend games and experience the excitement of live sports.

My passion for photography goes beyond capturing action—I love telling the stories behind the game.
